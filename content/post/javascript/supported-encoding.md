date: 2020-09-09T10:19:07+08:00
tags: ["javascript"]

- `ascii`
- `utf8`
- `utf16le`
- `ucs2`: alias of `utf16le`
- `base64`
- `latin1`
- `binary`: alias for `latin1`
- `hex`
